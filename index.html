About Our Portal

SkillIntern is a dedicated platform designed to bridge the gap between students seeking valuable work experience and companies looking for fresh talent. Inspired by the PM Internship Yojana, we aim to provide a streamlined, user-friendly experience for finding and posting internships nationwide.

Eligibility & Benefits

Who is Eligible?

Students

Currently enrolled in any recognized university or college.

Recent Graduates

Graduated within the last 2 years and looking for experience.

Skill Seekers

Anyone looking to learn new skills through practical work.

Indian Citizens

Must be a citizen of India to apply for listed opportunities.

What are the Benefits?

Real-World Experience

Gain hands-on experience in your field of interest.

Stipend

Earn a stipend while you learn and contribute.

Networking

Connect with industry professionals and build your network.

Certification

Receive a certificate upon successful completion of the internship.
